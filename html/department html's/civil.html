Civil Technology

A desirable small house, to decorate it as beautifully as possible, has been a life-long endeavor of man since time immemorial. World skyscrapers are being built, how many millions of dollars are being spent on construction, yet people's desire is not met. The Taj Mahal of Agra is still remembered as a world-class architectural feat. The techniques that people have discovered in the construction of communication infrastructure, housing infrastructure, planned cities, these strategists today are considered as civil engineers. Civil engineers/technologists do all the work of planning, estimating, designing etc. in buildings, roads, bridges, culverts, dams, water works, sewerage works, plumbing, material testing, soil testing, hydrological works, surveying etc. Moreover, the field level works are carried out in supervision, quality control, cost accounting, mentoring and control of construction workers. Public Works Department, Roads and Highways Department, Water Development Board, Power Development Board, LGED, Facilities, Railways etc. got initial appointment as Sub-Assistant Engineer (Assistant Engineer in some cases) in Government, Semi-Government and Private Institutions. Civil engineering diploma graduates have a wide range of jobs in the construction contracting business. Civil engineering diploma graduates have huge employment opportunities in many countries of the world including Middle East, South East Asia. Main Subjects: Civil Engineering, Drawing, Estimating, Civil Engineering Materials and Material Testing, Soil Mechanics, Structural Mechanics, Theory and Design of Structures, Hydraulics, Highways and Railways, Environmental Engineering, Construction Process, Surveying etc. Civil engineering education can be taken as a challenge to build a prosperous life.
