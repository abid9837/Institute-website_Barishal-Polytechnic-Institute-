Power Technology

Power technology is one of the eight technologies currently being taught in Barisal Polytechnic Institute. While studying in this technology a student will be exposed to (a) Automobile Engine and Power Transmission System and Body Building (b) Steam Generation (Boilers), Steam Engines and Steam Turbines, (c) Pumps, Hydraulics Machines, Fluid Mechanics and Air Compressors, (d) Electricity Generation , Transmission and Distribution, (e) Lifting Tackles, Hoists and Mechanical Machine Tools etc. are to be educated in major areas. After passing Diploma Engineering in Power Technology there are wide scope of self employment apart from getting appointed as Assistant Engineer in Govt, Semi Govt, Autonomous Institutions, Private Organizations, NGO's especially as Assistant Engineer. Among the institutions that have employment opportunities are: Junior Instructor in Polytechnic Institute and Sub-Assistant Engineer in Second Class, Public Works Department, Roads and Highways Department, Housing and Settlements, Power Development Board, Water Development Board, Food and Sugar Industries Corporation, Chemical Industries Corporation, Steel and Engineering Corporation, BADC, Rural Electrification Board, Rural Electricity Association, T&T Department etc. The students of this department are well versed in both electrical and mechanical technology due to the curriculum of Power Technology combining various subjects of Electrical Technology and Mechanical Technology. Graduates from this technology are employed abroad with great skill and reputation and contribute to building a strong national economic base by earning a lot of foreign exchange.

Shops and Labs of Power Technology:

1. Power Shop

2. Refrigeration and air conditioning shop

3. Hydraulics Lab

4. Heat Engine Lab
