Introduction Electromedical ,

Electromedical Technology

In order to live a healthy, normal and prosperous life, people should take care of their bodies along with social activities and be free from diseases. At present, due to various reasons, the use of medical science and modern medical equipment is continuously increasing due to the increasing number of people's physical ailments. When a person is sick, there is no substitute for medical technology equipment. For example, when a person is sick, he is given various tests. And these tests require different devices or equipment. And the operation, maintenance and repair of all these equipments are the work of electromedical technology. Students in this technology are Human Anatomy and Physiology, Biomedical Engineering, Clinical Chemistry, Medical Transducers and Sensors, Operating Room Equipment and Safety, Biomedical Signal Processing, Biomedical Instrumentation and Measurement, Dental Equipment, Anesthesia and Respiratory Equipment, Coronary and Intensive Care Equipment, Reza Int. Acquires knowledge and skills in electrical and electronics circuits and machines for operating all medical equipment including medical field, robotics surgery, radiology, physiotherapy and rehabilitation equipment, diagnostic and laboratory equipment, medical physics and nuclear instruments, microcontroller and embedded biomedical equipment. Diploma engineers who pass in this technology get employment opportunities in various public and private hospitals, diagnostic centers and clinics. Besides, there are opportunities to work in medical equipment manufacturing companies and establish medical equipment supply companies as entrepreneurs in operations, maintenance and servicing.
