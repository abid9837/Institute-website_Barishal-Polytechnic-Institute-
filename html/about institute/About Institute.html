Barishal Polutechnic Institute

Brief Introduction of the Institute

The journey of Barisal Polytechnic Institute started in 1962 with "Civil" and "Power" technology. The then government of Pakistan established 16 polytechnic institutes in the district headquarters of East Pakistan. Barisal Polytechnic Institute is one of them. Later, mechanical technology was introduced in 1968, "electrical" technology in 1981. “Electronics” Technology in 1994, “Computers” in 2002 and Electromedical Technology in 2006 and Tourism and Hospitality Management in 2016. Currently, a total of 1600 students are admitted in 08 technologies every year. The large infrastructural institution spread over 12.5 acres of land offers quality technical and vocational education and training through administration-cum-academic building-1, academic building-2, workshops/laboratories and well-utilized state-of-the-art machines and equipment. 220 students in 1 hostel and 64 students in 1 female residence are getting education with best facilities including residential. The campus has accommodation for teaching staff. A well-stocked library with 35844 books for learning, a vast pond in front of the entrance gate, a mosque next to it, a large playground for sports, which contribute to the creation of intellectual and thoughtful human resources, enhance the beauty and value of the institute. The present government of Bangladesh has laid special importance on polytechnic education in the production of skilled human resources through technical and vocational education and training along with science and engineering education, Barisal Polytechnic Institute nurtures the importance of technical education and produces skilled human resources through diploma engineering graduates and short-term trade courses. It continues to play a leading role in the socio-economic development of the country.
